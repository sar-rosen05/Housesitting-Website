Services

Flexible care built around your home, your pets, and your travel schedule.

Overnight House Sitting

I stay overnight at your home for the duration of your trip, keeping it lived-in and secure while sticking to your normal household routine.

🐶

Pet Sitting & Care

Feeding, walks, playtime, litter boxes, and medication administration for dogs, cats, and other household pets — on their regular schedule.

🪴

Plant & Garden Care

Watering indoor and outdoor plants, light garden upkeep, and making sure everything is thriving by the time you're back.

Mail, Package & Trash Handling

Collecting mail and packages and rotating trash/recycling bins so your home never looks unattended.

🔒

Home Security Basics

Rotating lights and blinds, checking doors and windows, and maintaining a lived-in appearance throughout your trip.

📸

Regular Updates

Photo and text check-ins so you always know your home and pets are doing well while you're away.
