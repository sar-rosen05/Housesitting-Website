Good to Know

A few boundaries that keep things clear and comfortable for both of us, up front.

🏡

Overnight Stays Only

I don't offer kenneling at my own home, and I don't do single-day drop-in visits. Every booking is an overnight stay at your home, for a minimum of 3 consecutive nights — it's either that, or I'm not the right fit for the trip.

📷

Camera & Security Disclosure

Please let me know in advance if your home has security cameras. Outdoor cameras (Ring doorbells and similar) are no problem at all — I just ask to be told beforehand about any cameras inside the house or in bedrooms.

🚭

Smoke & Vape Free

I don't smoke or vape, in your home or anywhere on your property.

🚪

No Uninvited Guests

I don't have visitors over during a stay unless it's discussed and approved with you first.

🔒

Your Privacy, Respected

Your home's address and any identifiable photos of your property stay private — I don't post them publicly or share them on social media.

Time & Scheduling

Transparency on how much I'm in the home during a stay — so there are no surprises for either of us, and it's clear why pricing below has two base rates.

🗓️

Standard Presence

I'm home overnight and present for all scheduled feeding and care windows, but I do have flexibility to step out during the day for errands, appointments, or to see friends — typically no more than 6–8 hours away at a stretch. I'm always back to sleep at your home overnight.

🏠

Extended Presence

For pets or situations that need more company throughout the day — anxious pets, medical needs, young puppies — I can commit to being in or around your home for most of the day (8+ hours), with minimal time away.

Deposit & Cancellation Policy

Straightforward and fair — so both of us are protected once dates are confirmed.

Deposit

There's no charge for the consultation itself. To confirm your dates, a deposit of 20% of your total booking cost is collected in person at the end of your free consultation. This single payment covers my travel to and from your home — so there's no separate distance fee — and it's credited toward your total balance, not an added cost. The remaining balance is due when you arrive back home at the end of your trip.

Cancellation

Deposits are non-refundable. Once your dates are confirmed, I turn down other bookings for that window, so the deposit covers that commitment and my travel costs regardless of when a cancellation happens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers to the questions I hear most.

What areas do you serve?

The Greater Seattle Area, including Woodinville, Redmond, Kirkland, Seattle, Bellevue, Everett, and Lynnwood. I generally can't commit to areas more than 45 minutes from home, since I need to be able to get back to my own area for doctor's appointments or personal matters if something comes up during your trip.

Do you offer single-night stays or drop-in visits?

No — every booking is an overnight stay at your home, for a minimum of 3 consecutive nights. I don't offer kenneling at my own home or single-day drop-ins.

How do I book a stay?

Start with a free consultation — no obligation. If you'd like to move forward, a 20% deposit is collected at the end of that visit to lock in your dates.

What if I need to cancel?

Deposits are non-refundable, since I turn down other bookings once your dates are confirmed. See Pricing & Policies for full details.

Will I get updates while I'm away?

Yes — I send photo and text updates throughout your stay so you always know how things are going.

What's the difference between Standard and Extended Presence?

Standard Presence (included in the base rate) means I'm home overnight and for all feeding/care windows, with some daytime flexibility to run errands. Extended Presence is an upgraded tier for pets or situations that need me home most of the day. See Pricing & Policies for rates.

Do you offer a discount for longer stays?

Yes — stays of 5+ consecutive nights get a 10% discount.

What if my home has security cameras?

Just let me know in advance. Outdoor cameras (like Ring doorbells) are totally fine — I ask to be told beforehand about any cameras inside the house or in bedrooms.
