About Sarah's Housesitting

Local, trustworthy care for your home and pets in the Greater Seattle Area.

Hi, I'm Sarah 👋

I've been housesitting since I graduated high school, and I've loved every bit of it. I know how hard it is to leave home and truly relax when you're worried about pets, plants, and an empty house. I treat every home I stay in and every pet I care for exactly like my own.

Washington has felt like home ever since I moved here — this is the community I've chosen to put down roots in, and I care about the people in it. At home, I share my life with two Great Pyrenees and a flock of 20–30 chickens, so caring for animals isn't just a job for me, it's part of my everyday life. I bring that same organized, detail-oriented mindset into every home I look after — I work with homeowners who want more than a quick drop-in, someone who actually stays, keeps a routine, and sends updates so you can enjoy your trip without worrying.

Before starting this business, I spent years as a lifeguard and swim instructor, trusted with keeping kids safe every day, and I've helped run day-to-day operations for a family business — bookkeeping, inventory, logistics, all of it. That same reliability and attention to detail is exactly what I bring into your home.
