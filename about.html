About Sarah's Housesitting

Local, trustworthy care for your home and pets in the Greater Seattle Area.

Hi, I'm Sarah 👋

I started Sarah's Housesitting because I know how hard it is to leave home and truly relax when you're worried about pets, plants, and an empty house. I treat every home I stay in and every pet I care for exactly like my own.

Based in the Greater Seattle Area, I work with homeowners who want more than a quick drop-in — someone who actually stays, keeps a routine, and sends updates so you can enjoy your trip without worrying.

Whether it's a short weekend trip or an extended vacation, I bring reliability, clear communication, and genuine care for your space and your pets.
